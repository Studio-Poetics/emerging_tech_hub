Foundational Papers

"Quantum Mechanical Computers" (1985)

By Richard Feynman
The seminal paper where Feynman argued that simulation of quantum physics would require a quantum computer.

"Nature isn't classical, dammit, and if you want to make a simulation of nature, you'd better make it quantum mechanical."

"A Fast Quantum Mechanical Algorithm for Database Search" (1996)

By Lov Grover
The paper introducing Grover's Algorithm, proving quantum computers could search unsorted databases quadratically faster than classical ones.
