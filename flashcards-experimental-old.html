What is the fundamental concept behind machine learning?

01 Foundation
Core Concept
Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables computers to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed for every task.
Key Principle
Instead of following pre-programmed instructions, ML systems identify patterns in data and use these patterns to make predictions or decisions on new, unseen data.
Example
Email spam detection: The system learns from thousands of emails labeled as "spam" or "not spam" to automatically classify new incoming emails.
