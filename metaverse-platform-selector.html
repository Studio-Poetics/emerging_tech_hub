üìã Before You Start

Choosing the right metaverse platform depends on multiple factors: your project goals, technical skills, budget, and timeline. This tool evaluates 5 popular platforms based on your specific needs.

Platforms Evaluated:

  • Mozilla Hubs: Web-based, no download, best for quick meetings/education
  • Spatial: High-end visuals, professional presentations, NFT galleries
  • Frame VR: Simple 3D gallery builder, portfolio showcases
  • VRChat: Deep customization via Unity, massive social community
  • Roblox: Game development platform, younger demographic

How The Scoring Works:

Each platform starts with a base score. Based on your answers, platforms gain or lose points across 5 dimensions:

  • Use Case Fit: How well the platform matches your project type
  • Budget Alignment: Whether the platform fits your financial constraints
  • Technical Accessibility: Matches your technical skill level
  • Visual Requirements: Meets your visual quality expectations
  • Audience Access: Installation requirements vs. ease of access

üí° Pro Tip: There's no single "best" platform. Mozilla Hubs might score highest for education, while VRChat excels for custom social experiences. Focus on your #1 recommendation's reasoning.
