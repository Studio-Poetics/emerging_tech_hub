Technology Readiness Levels (TRL)
Technology Readiness Levels are a systematic metric used to assess the maturity of blockchain technologies. For blockchain, TRL assessment considers factors like network security, transaction throughput, smart contract reliability, consensus mechanism stability, and real-world adoption across diverse use cases.
