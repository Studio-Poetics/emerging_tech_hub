Technology Readiness Levels (TRL)
Technology Readiness Levels for Future Interfaces & Next-Gen HCI assess maturity from basic research to widespread deployment. TRL evaluation considers interaction accuracy, ergonomics, latency, hardware miniaturization, regulatory/safety clearance, and real-world adoption across consumer, enterprise, and clinical contexts — from touchscreens to brain-computer interfaces.
