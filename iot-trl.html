Technology Readiness Levels (TRL)
Technology Readiness Levels for IoT assess the maturity of connected device technologies, connectivity protocols, data platforms, and deployment ecosystems. TRL evaluation considers hardware reliability, network coverage, power efficiency, security standards, interoperability, and real-world operational scale across consumer, industrial, and smart city applications.
