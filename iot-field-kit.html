Development Boards

The brain of your IoT system. These microcontroller boards handle sensors, connectivity, and logic.

ESP32 DevKit V1

₹200-600 Beginner Friendly WiFi + BLE

The go-to board for most IoT projects. Built-in WiFi and Bluetooth, low power consumption, Arduino-compatible, and incredibly affordable.

  • Dual-core 240MHz processor
  • Built-in WiFi (802.11 b/g/n) & Bluetooth 4.2
  • 30+ GPIO pins for sensors/actuators
  • Deep sleep mode (10μA) for battery projects
  • Arduino IDE & MicroPython support

Best For: Smart home devices, sensor networks, WiFi-enabled projects

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹299), ThinkRobotics (₹349), Amazon India (₹399)

ESP32-C6 DevKit

₹500-900 WiFi 6 RISC-V

The next-generation ESP32 with WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.3, and RISC-V architecture. Better performance, lower power, and future-proof connectivity.

  • Single-core RISC-V processor (160MHz)
  • WiFi 6 (802.11ax) with 2.4GHz support
  • Bluetooth 5.3 LE with Mesh support
  • 22 GPIO pins, ultra-low power (7μA deep sleep)
  • Zigbee & Thread protocol support
  • ESP-IDF & Arduino support

Best For: Battery-powered devices, Matter smart home, mesh networks, future-proof projects

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹649), ThinkRobotics (₹749), Amazon India (₹799)

Arduino Uno R3

₹400-800 Beginner Friendly USB Powered

The classic learning board. Simple, reliable, and supported by thousands of tutorials. Perfect for understanding sensor basics before adding connectivity.

  • ATmega328P microcontroller (16MHz)
  • 14 digital I/O pins, 6 analog inputs
  • 5V operation, USB programming
  • Massive community & library support
  • Shields available for WiFi, GSM, LoRa

Best For: Learning electronics, sensor prototyping, classroom projects

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹549), Amazon India (₹650), Electroncomponents (₹599)

Raspberry Pi Pico W

₹600-900 WiFi Enabled Python & C

Modern microcontroller with built-in WiFi. More powerful than Arduino, cheaper than full Raspberry Pi. Great for Python developers entering IoT.

  • Dual-core ARM Cortex-M0+ (133MHz)
  • Built-in WiFi (2.4GHz 802.11n)
  • 26 GPIO pins, 3 analog inputs
  • MicroPython & C/C++ support
  • 264KB RAM for complex programs

Best For: Python developers, data logging, multi-sensor systems

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹649), ThinkRobotics (₹699), Rarecomponents (₹750)

NodeMCU ESP8266

₹150-350 Ultra Cheap WiFi

The most affordable WiFi-enabled board. Older than ESP32 but still excellent for simple connected projects.

  • 80MHz processor with WiFi
  • 128KB RAM, 4MB flash
  • 11 GPIO pins
  • MicroUSB programming
  • 3.3V operation

Best For: Budget WiFi projects, home automation, learning IoT connectivity

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹199), Amazon India (₹249), Electroncomponents (₹299)

Raspberry Pi 4 (2GB/4GB)

₹4,500-7,500 Advanced Full Linux

A full Linux computer for edge computing. Run databases, web servers, computer vision, and AI at the edge.

  • Quad-core ARM Cortex-A72 (1.5GHz)
  • 2GB/4GB/8GB RAM options
  • Dual 4K HDMI, USB 3.0, Gigabit Ethernet
  • 40-pin GPIO for sensors
  • Run Python, Node.js, databases, Docker

Best For: Edge gateways, computer vision, local data processing, AI inference

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹5,499 for 2GB), Amazon India (₹6,200 for 4GB)

Particle Photon 2

₹2,500-3,500 Production Ready Cloud Integrated

Professional-grade IoT board with built-in cloud connectivity, OTA updates, and cellular options. Built for products, not just prototypes.

  • ARM Cortex-M4F processor
  • Built-in WiFi & Bluetooth
  • Cloud IDE & OTA firmware updates
  • REST API and webhooks built-in
  • Certification-ready for commercial deployment

Best For: Commercial IoT products, fleet management, remote monitoring

Indian Vendors: Import from Particle.io, Mouser Electronics ships to India

Sensors & Actuators

The eyes, ears, and hands of your IoT system. Sensors collect data from the physical world; actuators change it.

DHT22 Temperature & Humidity Sensor

₹150-300 Beginner Digital

The most popular environmental sensor. Reliable, cheap, and simple to use. Perfect first sensor.

  • Temperature: -40°C to 80°C (±0.5°C accuracy)
  • Humidity: 0-100% RH (±2-5% accuracy)
  • Digital output, 1-wire protocol
  • 3-5V operation

Use Cases: Weather stations, greenhouse monitoring, home automation

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹199), Amazon India (₹249)

MQ-135 Air Quality Sensor

₹100-250 Beginner Analog

Detects CO2, ammonia, benzene, smoke, and other air pollutants. Crucial for Indian cities with air quality issues.

  • Detects: NH3, NOx, alcohol, benzene, smoke, CO2
  • Analog output (ADC required)
  • Heating time: 24-48 hours for calibration
  • 5V operation

Use Cases: Delhi/Mumbai air quality monitors, industrial safety, ventilation control

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹149), Electroncomponents (₹179)

Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor

₹150-350 Agriculture Analog

Measures soil water content without corrosion. Essential for precision farming and irrigation automation.

  • Capacitive sensing (no probe corrosion)
  • Analog output (0-3.3V)
  • Waterproof construction
  • 3-5.5V operation

Use Cases: Smart irrigation, greenhouse farming, agricultural research

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹249), ThinkRobotics (₹299)

HC-SR501 PIR Motion Sensor

₹80-200 Beginner Digital

Detects human motion via infrared. Ultra-cheap, ultra-reliable. The heart of smart security systems.

  • Detection range: 3-7 meters
  • 120° detection angle
  • Digital HIGH when motion detected
  • Adjustable sensitivity & delay

Use Cases: Security systems, automatic lighting, occupancy detection

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹99), Amazon India (₹149)

HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Distance Sensor

₹70-180 Beginner Digital

Measures distance using ultrasonic pulses. Perfect for parking sensors, tank level monitoring, and obstacle detection.

  • Range: 2cm to 4 meters
  • Accuracy: ±3mm
  • 15° beam angle
  • 5V operation, 2mA current

Use Cases: Water tank monitoring, parking assistance, waste bin fill detection

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹89), Amazon India (₹129)

NEO-6M GPS Module

₹350-700 Intermediate UART

Add precise location tracking to your IoT device. Essential for vehicle tracking, asset monitoring, and outdoor deployments.

  • 50-channel GPS receiver
  • 2.5m positional accuracy
  • UART output (NMEA protocol)
  • Built-in EEPROM, battery backup
  • -161 dBm sensitivity

Use Cases: Fleet tracking, delivery monitoring, agricultural equipment tracking

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹449), ThinkRobotics (₹549)

ACS712 Current Sensor (5A/20A/30A)

₹120-300 Industrial Analog

Measure AC/DC current without breaking the circuit. Monitor power consumption, detect equipment failures, prevent overload.

  • Hall-effect current sensing
  • Versions: 5A, 20A, 30A
  • Analog voltage output
  • Electrical isolation (safe)

Use Cases: Energy monitoring, predictive maintenance, solar panel monitoring

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹199), Electroncomponents (₹249)

4-Channel 5V Relay Module

₹180-400 Actuator Digital Control

Control high-voltage AC appliances with low-voltage microcontrollers. Turn lights, motors, pumps on/off remotely.

  • 4 independent relays
  • Control 220V AC or 30V DC (10A max)
  • Optocoupler isolation (safe)
  • LED indicators per channel

Use Cases: Home automation, irrigation control, industrial equipment switching

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹249), Amazon India (₹299)

Communication Modules

How your devices talk to each other and the cloud. Choose based on range, power, bandwidth, and cost requirements.

LoRa SX1278 433MHz Module

₹400-800 Long Range Low Power

Long-range communication up to 10km in open areas. Perfect for rural deployments, agriculture, and city-wide sensor networks.

  • Range: 2-10km (line of sight)
  • Low power: 100mA TX, 10mA RX
  • 433MHz (license-free in India)
  • SPI interface
  • LoRaWAN compatible

Use Cases: Smart agriculture, rural IoT, city-wide air quality networks

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹549), ThinkRobotics (₹649)

NRF24L01+ 2.4GHz Transceiver

₹100-250 Short Range Low Cost

Cheap wireless communication for sensor networks within buildings. Create mesh networks of sensors without WiFi.

  • Range: 30-100 meters
  • 2.4GHz ISM band
  • 2Mbps data rate
  • Ultra-low power (26μA standby)
  • Multi-device networks (6 pipes)

Use Cases: Wireless sensor networks, remote controls, home automation

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹129), Amazon India (₹179)

SIM800L GSM/GPRS Module

₹350-700 Cellular SMS + Data

Connect anywhere with cellular coverage. Send SMS alerts, make HTTP requests over 2G. Perfect for remote monitoring.

  • 2G GSM/GPRS connectivity
  • Send/receive SMS
  • HTTP requests over GPRS
  • Voice calls (with speaker/mic)
  • Works with any Indian SIM (Jio 2G not supported)

Use Cases: Remote locations without WiFi, SMS alerts, vehicle tracking

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹449), Amazon India (₹599), Note: Use Airtel/Vodafone SIM

XBee S2C Zigbee Module

₹1,200-2,000 Mesh Network Industrial

Industrial-grade mesh networking. Self-healing networks with hundreds of nodes. Used in smart homes and factories.

  • Mesh networking (up to 65,000 nodes)
  • Range: 120m indoor, 1200m outdoor
  • Low power (1μA sleep mode)
  • 128-bit AES encryption
  • Self-healing routes

Use Cases: Smart factories, building automation, large sensor deployments

Indian Vendors: Import from Digi.com, Mouser ships to India

HC-05 Bluetooth Module

₹200-400 Short Range Simple

Classic Bluetooth for phone-to-device communication. Great for configuration, debugging, and short-range control.

  • Bluetooth 2.0 (classic)
  • Range: 10 meters
  • UART interface (easy serial)
  • Master or slave mode
  • Android/iOS compatible

Use Cases: Phone-controlled devices, wireless debugging, proximity sensors

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹249), Amazon India (₹299)

W5500 Ethernet Module

₹400-800 Wired Reliable

Wired network connectivity for industrial reliability. No WiFi interference, secure, and always-on connectivity.

  • 10/100 Mbps Ethernet
  • Hardwired TCP/IP stack
  • 8 independent sockets
  • SPI interface to MCU
  • No packet loss, deterministic latency

Use Cases: Industrial automation, critical infrastructure, factory IoT

Indian Vendors: Robu.in (₹549), ThinkRobotics (₹649)

Buying Tips for Indian Makers

  • Start Small: Begin with a starter kit (ESP32 + sensors bundle, ~₹1,500) before buying components individually
  • Check Reviews: Verify seller ratings on Amazon/Flipkart for component authenticity
  • Power Supplies: Buy quality 5V/3.3V power supplies—cheap ones damage boards
  • GSM/LTE Modules: Verify SIM compatibility (Jio doesn't support 2G; use Airtel/Vodafone)
  • Import Duties: For orders >₹2,000 from international vendors, expect 42% customs + GST
  • Clone vs. Original: Arduino clones work fine and cost 50% less; ESP32 clones are reliable too
  • Shipping: Robu/ThinkRobotics have better packaging than Amazon for fragile sensors
  • Bulk Discounts: Email vendors directly for student/institution discounts (10-20% off)
