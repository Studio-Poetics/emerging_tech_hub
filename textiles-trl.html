Technology Readiness Levels (TRL)
Technology Readiness Levels for Smart Textiles & E-Textiles assess maturity from basic research to widespread deployment. TRL evaluation considers material durability, washability, manufacturing scalability, sensing accuracy, power/energy requirements, regulatory compliance, and real-world performance across diverse applications including fashion, sportswear, healthcare, and defense contexts.
