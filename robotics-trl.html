Technology Readiness Levels (TRL)
Technology Readiness Levels for Robotics assess maturity from basic research to operational deployment. TRL evaluation considers hardware reliability, software autonomy, safety certifications, environmental adaptability, human-robot interaction quality, and real-world performance in industrial, commercial, and consumer applications.
